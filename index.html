A minimal starter stylesheet for blog.

What is this?

This? It's a stylesheet that provides basic and beautiful styles for a simple blogs. Unlike the vast majority of popular CSS libraries (*ahem* Bootstrap), this doesn't depend on a class-based system and mess up your HTML.
